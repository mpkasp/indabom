The Origin Story

Hi! I'm Mike. I built IndaBOM as a side project to support the hardware development going on at my last startup, Atlas Wearables (Austin, TX, USA).

When we first started Atlas, like many, we were just using spreadsheets. We quickly outgrew this and moved on to using a bulky Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) tool. Paying large amounts per seat, per month for a tool that we only utilized a fraction of the features got old fast. Not to mention that the added complexity required an additional part-time consultant to assist with! After our first project, we cancelled that subscription and we were back to square one.
